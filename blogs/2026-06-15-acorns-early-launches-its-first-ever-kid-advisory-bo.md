Acorns Early launched its inaugural Kid Advisory Board, a founding group of four young members aged 7-15 with backgrounds in entrepreneurship, activism, content creation, and investing, to help shape future product development for the family finance platform. The announcement cites that 62% of parents lack the confidence to teach financial literacy.
